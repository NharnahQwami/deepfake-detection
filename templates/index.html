Understanding Deepfakes

What are Deepfakes?

Deepfakes are synthetic media where a person's image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness using artificial intelligence and deep learning technology.

Why Detection Matters

As deepfake technology becomes more sophisticated, the ability to detect manipulated media is crucial for maintaining trust in digital content and preventing misinformation.

How It Works

Verify Sources
